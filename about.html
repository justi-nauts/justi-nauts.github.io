My Hometown

Greensboro Georgia is a small town of around 3,603 citizens founded in the year 1786. It is named after the Revolutionary War freedom fighter General Nathanael Greene. Once land owned by the Creek Indians between the Oconee and Ogeechee Rivers, now Greensboro, was first known as a small frontier settlement called Greenesborough. My parents moved to Greensboro around 2010 to start a fullfillment company. The town is ismall, with few people and amenities surrounding a beautiful lake. We have lived in the town ever since and enjoy the scenery and calm atmosphere

Sports

Sports have always been an important part of my family. My father, Scott Zahler was a baseball player in college and was drafted to the MLB by the Cincinatti Reds. Although he never made it to the show, he played professionally in Paris for a few years before becoming a lawyer. I myself played Tennis all of my life and won 2x State Championships in high school.
